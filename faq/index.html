1. Who are we?

Messe Worldwide is a full service agency specialising in events and exhibitions. We identify world class trade shows that meet your requirements so that we can provide you with a range of tailored services while you can concentrate on the things that matter.

2. What we do? / What we service?

On top of organising exhibitions, we also offer a range of services that can be tailored to your requirements. From travel to logistics management, we work with our service partners to offer you world class service right here in Malaysia.

5. How to claim government incentives?

Information for Exporters

The Government of Malaysia has various initiatives that will assist you as an exporter. Some of the most commonly used initiatives are:

  1. Market Development Grant (MDG)
    The MDG is a support initiative in the form of a reimbursable grant. For more details, please visit here. Terms and conditions apply.
  2. Tax Incentives under the Double Deduction for Promotion of Export
    This is a tax incentive allowed for companies going for export. For more details on the expenses that qualify for double deduction, please visit here. Please consult your tax agent for more information.
